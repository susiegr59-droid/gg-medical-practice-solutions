Important boundaries

G&G provides administrative, billing, authorization, and payer-process support. We do not provide medical, legal, tax, or clinical advice, and we cannot guarantee payer approval. When direct payer or provider contact is needed, written authorization may be required before G&G can act on someone else's behalf.
