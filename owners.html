SG

Co-Founder | Strategy, Innovation & Practice Growth

Susie Gruber

Susie Gruber is the Co-Founder of G&G Medical Practice Solutions, LLC, where she leads strategic growth, business development, innovation, and long-term planning. With more than two decades of healthcare leadership experience, Susie has dedicated her career to helping physician practices improve operational performance, strengthen financial stability, and navigate the increasingly complex business of healthcare.

Her career has spanned nearly every level of medical practice administration — from billing specialist and office manager to practice manager, senior revenue cycle leader, and business owner. That hands-on experience has provided her with a practical understanding of the operational, financial, and regulatory challenges practices face every day.

Early in her career, Susie was among the first 200 professionals in the United States to earn the Certified Professional Coder designation. While her career has long since expanded beyond coding, that foundation continues to influence her approach to compliance, reimbursement strategy, payer policy, and operational excellence.

As founder of JLG Medical Billing LLC, Susie built a reputation for delivering far more than traditional billing services. She has partnered with physicians and healthcare organizations to improve workflows, optimize reimbursement, strengthen payer relationships, enhance compliance, and develop sustainable business strategies that support long-term practice growth.

Susie is also the creator of ProSuite, an advanced practice management software platform currently being developed through a separate company. Designed to simplify credentialing, compliance, revenue cycle oversight, provider management, reporting, and practice operations, ProSuite reflects her passion for combining real-world healthcare expertise with practical technology solutions. As the platform evolves, opportunities may arise for collaboration with or integration into G&G Medical Practice Solutions, LLC.

Throughout her career, Susie has lived by a simple philosophy: give 110% and always go the extra mile. She believes exceptional service is earned by anticipating challenges, solving problems proactively, and consistently exceeding client expectations.

JG

Co-Founder | Operations & Practice Performance

Jen Girouard

Jen Girouard serves as Co-Founder of G&G Medical Practice Solutions, LLC, where she helps healthcare organizations improve efficiency, strengthen compliance, and optimize every stage of the revenue cycle. Her ability to combine technical expertise with exceptional organization and responsiveness makes her an invaluable resource for practices seeking dependable operational support.

Prior to co-founding G&G Medical Practice Solutions, LLC, Jen founded Mountain Medical Billing LLC, where she earned a reputation for helping physician practices streamline operations, resolve complex billing challenges, and improve payer compliance through practical, results-driven solutions.

Her expertise includes medical billing, practice management, revenue cycle management, credentialing, provider enrollment, prior authorizations, referral coordination, accounts receivable management, administrative workflows, and bookkeeping. She has supported providers across pain management, chiropractic, acupuncture, physical therapy, orthopedics, and primary care.

Jen is recognized for her strong work ethic, attention to detail, and ability to resolve complex reimbursement issues quickly and accurately. Her knowledge of payer policies, denials management, workflow optimization, and financial operations helps practices reduce administrative burden while improving performance.

Before entering healthcare administration, Jen developed a strong foundation in bookkeeping and accounting, giving her a well-rounded understanding of how clinical care, administrative workflows, and financial operations intersect. Her experience spans both small private practices and large hospital organizations with multiple facilities, allowing her to adapt quickly to different operational environments and payer expectations.

As a licensed Emergency Medical Technician, Jen also brings valuable clinical insight to her administrative and revenue cycle work. Her understanding of patient care, provider documentation, and healthcare operations strengthens her ability to support providers and bridge the gap between clinical and administrative teams.
