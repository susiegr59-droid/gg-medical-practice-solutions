Meet Susie Gruber

SG

Co-Owner, G&G Medical Practice Solutions, LLC

Susie Gruber

Susie brings more than 30 years of healthcare experience in medical billing, revenue cycle management, credentialing, practice operations, and accounts receivable recovery. Before co-founding G&G, she founded JLG Medical Billing LLC and built her work around helping practices improve reimbursement, reduce billing delays, strengthen workflows, and stay organized across payer requirements.

Her background includes pain management, spine, primary care, Medicaid programs, practice management, billing leadership, project coordination, and staff support. She is known for practical problem-solving, persistent payer follow-up, and helping practices bring order to complicated billing and administrative processes.

Susie is also the founder of The Hiraeth Group LLC, a parent software and technology company created to support long-term practice operations tools. Its flagship platform, ProSuite, is currently in testing and is being shaped around credentialing, licensing, recredentialing, provider management, reporting, and other administrative workflows medical practices need to manage with confidence.
